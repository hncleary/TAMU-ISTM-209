My experience at A&M University has been great. I love the area, the people, and the classes that I'm taking. I've met quite a few new people and had a lot of fun since I first first got here. I even managed to learn a bit while I was here as well. Even though I'm excited to graduate soon, I really will miss this place and the time in my life that it took up.

Studying and working for my Computer Science degree takes up quite a bit of my time. Even though I'm often occupied with lectures, math work, and coding I still find time for small hobbies that I very much enjoy. In my free time that isn't spent with friends I try to learn about new creative software suites. As mentioned on the home page of this site, I use these pieces of software to make humorous photos and videos that I share online with the public. Currently, I'm focused on improving my skills in 3D animation. I hope to be able to use all my skills to create a 3D short film in the near future.
